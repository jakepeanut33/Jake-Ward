# Snugpaw — Pet Dropshipping Store Kit 🐾

A complete starter kit to launch a cozy/personalized **pet** dropshipping brand,
run Facebook/Meta ads, and operate the store — built from 2026 trend research.

This repo contains two things:

### 1. `docs/` — the full playbook
Step-by-step business plan, product research, Shopify setup, ready-to-paste product
listings, a complete Facebook ads strategy (with ad copy + creative briefs),
financials, and a launch checklist. **Start at [`docs/README.md`](docs/README.md).**

### 2. `storefront/` — a deployable Next.js storefront
A fast, conversion-focused brand site and ad-landing pages (Next.js 14 + Tailwind),
with checkout handed off to your real Shopify store. See
[`storefront/README.md`](storefront/README.md).

```bash
cd storefront && npm install && npm run dev   # http://localhost:3000
```

## What this kit does and doesn't do

✅ Gives you a researched niche (cozy/personalized pet goods), 6 vetted trending
products with real margins, a built storefront, all your copy and policies, and an
exact Facebook ads plan calibrated to a $150–$500/month budget.

❌ It can't create your Shopify or Meta accounts, enter your payment details, sign up
suppliers, or spend your ad budget — those require **your** accounts, identity, and
agreement to each platform's terms (and you generally must be 18+ to advertise on
Meta; if you're a minor, an adult needs to own the accounts). The playbook walks you
through every one of those steps.

## Suggested order

1. Read [`docs/README.md`](docs/README.md), then files 1 → 7 in `docs/`.
2. Build the Shopify store (`docs/03`) and paste in the listings (`docs/04`).
3. Set up tracking + run ads (`docs/05`), watching the numbers (`docs/06`).
4. Optionally deploy the `storefront/` app as your ad landing pages.
5. Work the [launch checklist](docs/07-launch-checklist.md) before spending on ads.

> Dropshipping is a real business with real costs and a learning curve — not
> free money. Budget your first ~$300 of ad spend as tuition, test honestly, and
> double down on what works.
