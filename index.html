About Western Forms

Built in the heartland.
Trusted on six continents.

For more than six decades, Western Forms has manufactured the aluminum forming systems that builders rely on to pour concrete faster and more affordably. From our Kansas City facility, we ship complete, engineered systems anywhere a structure needs to rise.

Every form is precision-machined, infinitely reusable, and backed by a team that has been solving forming problems since the Eisenhower administration.
