# 7 · Launch Checklist

Don't spend a dollar on ads until every box is checked.

## Foundation
- [ ] Store name + logo + favicon set
- [ ] Custom domain connected (not `*.myshopify.com`)
- [ ] Theme customized, looks great **on mobile**
- [ ] Brand colors + announcement bar ("Free shipping over $45 · 30-day guarantee")

## Products
- [ ] All products imported from supplier and connected for auto-fulfillment
- [ ] Real images on every product (ideally your own sample photos)
- [ ] Titles, descriptions, prices, compare-at prices set (file 4)
- [ ] Personalization fields work (photo upload / name / phone)
- [ ] You **ordered a sample** of your hero product and it's good quality

## Trust
- [ ] Reviews app installed + some honest reviews showing
- [ ] About + Contact pages live with a real support email
- [ ] Shipping, Returns, Privacy, Terms pages live (templates provided)
- [ ] Realistic shipping times stated clearly on product + checkout
- [ ] Trust badges near the buy button

## Payments & ops
- [ ] Shopify Payments and/or PayPal active and **tested**
- [ ] Taxes configured for your region
- [ ] Shipping rates set (flat rate + free over $45)
- [ ] Placed a full **test order** end-to-end; confirmed supplier routing
- [ ] Order + shipping notification emails branded and working

## Tracking (critical for ads)
- [ ] Meta Business Manager + Ad Account + payment method
- [ ] Facebook Page (and Instagram) created with a few posts
- [ ] Meta Pixel + Conversions API installed via the Shopify Facebook app
- [ ] Events firing: ViewContent, AddToCart, InitiateCheckout, Purchase
- [ ] Domain verified in Business Settings
- [ ] Verified with Meta Pixel Helper that events actually fire

## Ads ready
- [ ] 3–5 creatives made per hero product (short captioned videos)
- [ ] Ad copy + headlines written (file 5)
- [ ] Campaign built: Sales objective, optimize for **Purchase**, broad targeting
- [ ] Daily budget + kill-rules decided (file 5/6)
- [ ] Break-even ROAS calculated and written down (file 6)

## Email (do in week 1)
- [ ] Klaviyo (or Shopify Email) installed
- [ ] Abandoned-cart flow on
- [ ] Welcome / first-order discount flow on

## Legal / responsibility
- [ ] An adult owns the payment + ad accounts if you're under 18
- [ ] Policy pages reviewed by an adult for your location
- [ ] No fake reviews, no fake scarcity, no medical claims
- [ ] Plan for sales tax / income reporting discussed with a parent

## After launch (first 2 weeks)
- [ ] Check Ads Manager daily; let ad sets gather ~$15–40 before judging
- [ ] Kill losers, note winning hooks
- [ ] Reply to every customer message within 24h
- [ ] Fulfill orders within 1–2 days
- [ ] Start collecting real reviews from buyers
- [ ] Track weekly numbers in your spreadsheet (file 6)
