# 2 · Product Research

## How these were chosen

I filtered 2026 trend data for products that are: (1) emotional / gift-driven, (2) in
the **$18–$40 retail sweet spot** (below ~$18, ad costs eat your margin), (3) capable
of **40–60% margins**, and (4) easy to ship. Personalized pet accessories and calming
beds led every list I checked.

Key facts from current sources (mid-2026):
- Phone accessories, **pet supplies**, and baby products are the fastest-growing
  online categories.
- **Personalized pet accessories** posted the strongest recent demand signal.
- **Donut/calming faux-fur beds:** ~45–55% margins. Pet accessories overall: 40–65%.
- Supplier unit costs typically **$3–$18**; price between **$18–$40**.
- Recommended suppliers: **Spocket** (fast US/EU), **Zendrop** (managed fulfillment),
  **CJ Dropshipping** (lowest fixed cost), plus **DSers** (AliExpress) and **TopDawg**.

## Why the two heroes

- **Custom Pet Portrait Blanket** — personalization = no price comparison, huge
  emotional pull, perfect for video ads ("turn your best friend into a keepsake").
  Great for gifting seasons and pet-memorial buyers.
- **Calming Donut Bed** — proven evergreen winner, clear "pain → solution" story
  (anxious pet → finally sleeps), strong margin, easy to demo on video.

## Where to source

1. **Start on Zendrop or DSers** to import quickly with a clean app inside Shopify.
2. For the **personalized blanket/tag**, you need a print-on-demand / custom supplier
   (search "custom pet blanket print on demand" on CJ, Zendrop, Printify, or Gelato).
   Personalized = made to order, so set shipping expectations (5–9 days US).
3. **Order samples of your 1–2 hero products yourself first.** You cannot write honest
   copy or judge quality from a photo. This is the single most-skipped, most-important
   step.

## How to validate a product before spending big

- **Demand:** is it selling on TikTok Shop / Amazon Best Sellers / AliExpress (1,000+
  orders)? Look it up.
- **Wow factor:** does a 5-second video make someone stop scrolling?
- **Margin:** retail ≥ 2.5–3× total cost (product + shipping). If not, skip it.
- **Not saturated to death:** some competition = proven demand (good). 50 identical
  ads running for a year = race to the bottom (bad). Differentiate with branding,
  bundles, or the personalization angle.
- **Not fragile / not problematic:** avoid glass, electronics with warranty issues,
  batteries (shipping rules), and anything making medical claims.

## Finding the *next* products (free tools)

- TikTok: search the niche + "tiktok made me buy it"; watch what's going viral.
- Facebook Ad Library (free): search competitors, see which ads they've run longest
  (long-running ad = profitable ad).
- Amazon Movers & Shakers / Best Sellers in Pet Supplies.
- AliExpress "orders" sort + DSers' "Find Suppliers."
- Paid (optional later): Minea, Sell The Trend, PiPiADS, Winning Hunter.
